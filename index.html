I have considered where I would like to head when I am qualified, and during this process, also considered a few places that I would not like to work. These ideas mainly revolve around my personal lifestyle choices, my interests and where I believe I may or may not be a particularly good cultural fit.
I am interested in Information Security and Privacy, and believe I could really blossom in some of the roles there. I am also interested in startup culture as a whole and would be open to a massive range of roles inside an early stage company.
An area I don’t believe I would suit is Social Media, this is mainly due to not personally maintaining a social media presence, so I don’t think I would bring the energy that I would want to bring to any role I held. I am open to everything, but believe that ultimately, being realistic with my traits and personal experiences will yield far better results for both my employer and myself.
I am looking for an honest and upfront company culture, I like to be honest and get straight to the source of issues. I would like to work in a workplace that is open minded and is as interested in my development as I am in theirs. I would absolutely love the opportunity to be mentored by a stronger/more experienced developer or engineer. I am looking for a workplace that treats their workers with respect whilst engaging in honest work.
